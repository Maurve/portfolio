Word

Microsoft Word is a word processor tool that was first released in October of 1983. It has many features that are very useful to those who use it no matter what the use is. The biggest feature that is used the most is the spell-checking tool. There is also a thesaurus and a dictionary too, so users won't have to look things up and can just use Word to find what they're looking for. There are over hundreds of templates that users can choose from, so they can have an attractive document that fits well with what they're creating/writing. It can be an essay or graphs that fill documents. WordArt is also a popular tool in Word. They can be used to create text with good-looking and attractive graphics like skewing, shadowing, rotating, and sketching.
